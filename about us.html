About WNX

West Nile Xtreme

Where Gaming Meets Community

WNX is more than just a tournament—it's a movement to bring together the gaming community across West Nile and beyond.

Our Mission

Using technology to forge strong ties between gamers, universities, and communities. We believe that gaming is a powerful tool for strengthening connections and bonds that transcend physical boundaries.

Connection

Bringing gamers together through competitive play and shared passion

Technology

Leveraging digital platforms to create seamless tournament experiences

Community

Building lasting relationships that extend beyond the game

Our Vision

To create the largest and most connected gaming community in Uganda, where every match is an opportunity to build friendships, showcase talent, celebrate our shared love and passion for the Game(FC26) and perhaps have fun.
